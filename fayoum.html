Fayoum

tourism in Fayoum

The city of Fayoum is one of the most important tourist cities in Egypt because of its ancient history throughout the ages. It includes many Roman and Pharaonic monuments, in addition to a group of museums, gardens, and wonderful valleys. Fayoum also has attractive tourism for visitors from inside and outside Egypt.

Hawara pyramid

It is one of the pharaonic pyramids and one of the most important tourist places in Fayoum. It was built for King Amenemhat III. It is made of mud bricks covered with a layer of limestone. Its original height was 58 meters. Only 20 meters remain. The pyramid of Hawara contains corridors and many rooms ending in a room. Burial.

Tunisia Village

The village of Tunis is one of the best tourist places in Fayoum, Egypt, which God has blessed with a beautiful nature and amazing views that made it a destination for visitors of various nationalities, as it combines the joyful green spaces, the blue waters of Lake Qarun, and the yellow sands north of the lake. It is also characterized by the manufacture of ceramics and a large number of The artistic ceremonies that make it an open-air museum, also includes the distinguished caricature museum.

The ancient city of Karnis

One of the best tourist attractions in Fayoum, Egypt, is one of the Greco-Roman cities established by Ptolemy III on an area of ​​800 meters. It is amazing that this entire city was discovered after excavations, and they found it to include houses built of mud bricks, whose walls were decorated with distinctive inscriptions, as well as a mill, a bakery , a granary, and two Roman baths, in addition to many temples, including the southern temple.

Qarun Palace

One of the most prestigious landmarks of the city of Fayoum, Egypt, is one of the Ptolemaic temples, known as the Temple of Ptolemy, which is characterized by its simple design, but carries within it the utmost architectural accuracy when the sun perpendicular every year to the Holy of Holies in this temple with the beginning of winter on September 21, this temple may seem From the outside it is small in size, but once you step inside it you will find an attractive place to discover its secrets.

Whale Valley Reserve

One of the most important tourist places in Fayoum, which is a nature reserve that contains structures of whales and other marine animals and plants dating back 40 million years, and the importance of Whale Valley is that it provides a natural environment for many endangered animals and birds, as well as for migratory birds throughout the year. It has mangrove forests.

Kom Ushim Archeology Museum

One of the most beautiful tourist places in Fayoum, which includes a unique collection of archaeological statues that bear the fragrance of history and the greatness of the Pharaohs, and distinctive paintings that are thousands of years old that tell us the legends of the distant past.
